Dining Notice

  • Buffet dining time is 90 minutes, calculated from your booked time slot. If a table is occupied beyond the allocated time, the restaurant may charge an extension fee of $15 per 15 minutes. Any period under 15 minutes will still be charged as 15 minutes.
  • Please take food according to your needs. When leaving, if leftover edible food exceeds 150g per person, the restaurant may charge a food waste handling fee of $15 per 150g. Any amount under 150g will not be charged separately.
  • To ensure quality and reduce waste, steak is cooked to order. Each guest may order 1 serving at a time, and may reorder once the previous serving is mostly finished.
  • Children under 1.2m are charged at 50% of the adult price. Children 1.2m and above are charged at the full adult price.
  • All buffet items are for dine-in only and may not be taken away. Smoking is not permitted on the premises. Outside food and drinks are not allowed.
  • Once a booking is confirmed, if fewer guests arrive than originally booked, reserved seats and the corresponding charges are not automatically refunded. If you wish to add guests, this will be subject to seat availability at the time. The restaurant reserves the right to adjust table arrangements based on the actual number of guests attending.
  • Paid bookings are held for 30 minutes from the reserved start time. If guests do not arrive within this time and have not contacted us in advance, the restaurant may release the table. If late arrival or non-arrival is due to the guest, refunds or rescheduling will follow the cancellation policy on this page.
  • For cancellations or rescheduling, please contact us at least 24 hours in advance. Requests made more than 24 hours before the booking time are eligible for a full refund. Cancellations made within 24 hours, no-shows, or cases without reasonable notice may incur deductions based on reserved seating, preparation, and actual loss, up to the full booking amount.
  • The restaurant will apply the rules on this page reasonably, in good faith, and in line with actual operational needs. In the event of a dispute, the basis of any charges will be explained to the guest.
